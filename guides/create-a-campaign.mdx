A **campaign** calls a list of contacts with one agent, on a schedule you set, with automatic retries. Set one up in the dashboard, or launch it from your CRM.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- An **audience** — your contact list. See [Audiences](#audiences) below.
- An **agent** to make the calls.
- A **phone number** — campaigns are outbound, so they need a provisioned number and a plan beyond the free trial. Email **admin@intrlume.tech** to have one added.

## Create a campaign

Go to **Campaigns → New** and follow the steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Campaign basics" icon="megaphone">
    Give the campaign a **name**, select one or more **audiences** (all their leads will be called), and pick the **agent**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the duration" icon="calendar">
    Choose a **start** and **end date** — calls only run inside this window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule & retries" icon="refresh-cw">
    Set the days, hours, and **timezone** calls may run, how often to **retry** (every few days), and the retry mode — **all** leads or **unanswered only**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate" icon="play">
    The campaign starts as a **draft**. Activate it from the campaign page when you're ready — then pause, resume, or stop anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Track progress and outcomes on the campaign page and in [Call Logs](/guides/view-call-logs).

## Audiences

An **audience** is the list of contacts a campaign calls. Build one once and reuse it across campaigns.

To create one, go to **Audiences → New**:

- Give it a **name** (and an optional description).
- **Upload a CSV** of contacts — Intrlume maps the columns (phone, name, and so on) and standardizes phone numbers. You can also **add contacts manually**.
- Any extra CSV columns become **custom fields** your agent can use on the call — a name, an account number, an amount due — referenced in prompts as `{cust_field}`.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Clean phone numbers and clear column headers import best; duplicates are flagged during upload.
</Callout>

## Campaigns in your CRM

You can launch a campaign without leaving your CRM. In a list view in **Zoho, Freshworks, or Bigin**, select the contacts, pick an agent and a schedule, and Intrlume runs the same paced campaign over them. Each result — disposition, summary, and recording — is written back to the matching record, so your CRM stays the source of truth.
