This guide takes you from zero to a working voice agent you can actually call.

## Before you start

<Callout kind="info">
  You need an Intrlume account. Sign in at [app.intrlume.tech](https://app.intrlume.tech) to begin — no phone hardware or coding required.
</Callout>

## 1. Create an agent

In the dashboard, go to **Agents → New** and use **Quick Create**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your agent" icon="edit-3">
    Give it a calling name and company, choose a voice and language (a primary language, plus an optional second), and describe the goal in plain language. For example:

    ```text
    Book solar demo appointments, confirm time slots, and handle rescheduling in Hindi and English.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate" icon="zap">
    Click **Generate**. Intrlume writes the prompt and greeting, picks settings, and builds the conversation flow in seconds. You can refine all of it later in the visual workflow editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Prefer a guided setup? Open **Chat** and let the assistant walk you through creating an agent step by step.
</Callout>

## 2. Test it

From the **Agents** list, try the agent before it ever talks to a real contact.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Test call" icon="phone">
    Click **Test Call** and enter your number — the agent rings your phone so you can talk to it live. Speak naturally; it handles interruptions and follows the flow you described.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web test link" icon="globe">
    Click **Generate Test Link** for a shareable, browser-based call (valid for about 30 minutes). Send it to a colleague to try without a phone.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

After a test call, open **Call Logs** to see the transcript, the recording, and any data the agent captured.

## 3. Launch outreach

Ready to call real contacts? Three short steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload an audience" icon="upload">
    Go to **Audiences → New** and upload a CSV of contacts. Intrlume maps the columns and standardizes phone numbers for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a campaign" icon="megaphone">
    Go to **Campaigns → New**, pick your audience and agent, set the calling window (days, hours, timezone) and retry rules, then activate the campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results" icon="bar-chart">
    Track progress on the campaign page, then dig into **Call Logs** and **Analytics** — answer rates, transcripts, outcomes, and extracted data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
