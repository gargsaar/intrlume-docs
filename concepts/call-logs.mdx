**Call Logs** is the record of every conversation your agents have — outbound, inbound, and web. Open any call to replay it, read it, and see what the agent captured.

## Find a call

Filter by phone number, agent, campaign, **direction** (outbound / inbound / web), **disposition** (answered, voicemail, no answer, busy, failed), date range, and duration.
