---
title: Knowledge Base
description: Give your agents your own content — PDFs, URLs, or text — so they answer accurately on the call.
---

A **knowledge base** is a set of documents your agent can search **during a call** to answer questions accurately, instead of guessing. Upload your content once, link it to an agent, and Intrlume retrieves the most relevant passages in real time.

## Add content three ways

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload a file" icon="file">
    Drop in a PDF, DOCX, or text file. Intrlume extracts and indexes the text automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From a URL" icon="link">
    Point to a web page and Intrlume fetches and indexes its content.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paste text" icon="edit-3">
    Paste FAQs, product details, or policies directly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest" icon="upload">
    Your content is split into small chunks and turned into embeddings for search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Index" icon="database">
    Status moves from *processing* to *ready* once it's searchable. Larger files take a little longer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retrieve on the call" icon="search">
    When a caller asks something, the agent searches the knowledge base and answers from the best-matching passages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Link it to an agent

Attach one or more knowledge bases to an [agent](/concepts/agents) from the workflow editor. The agent only pulls from what you link, so answers stay grounded in your content.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Keep documents focused and current — smaller, well-scoped sources retrieve more accurately than one giant file.
</Callout>
