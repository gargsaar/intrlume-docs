---
title: Evaluations
description: Automatic quality scoring of your calls — faithfulness, hallucination, and topic adherence.
---

**Evaluations** score the quality of your agent's conversations automatically, so you can trust what it says and catch problems early.

## What gets scored

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Faithfulness" icon="circle-check">
    How well the agent sticks to your content and instructions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hallucination" icon="alert-triangle">
    Whether the agent invented anything not backed by your content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Topic adherence" icon="target">
    Whether the conversation stayed on the intended goal.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## How you use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn it on per agent" icon="toggle-right">
    Enable evaluation for an agent so its calls are scored.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run or review" icon="flask-conical">
    Score live calls, or run a test evaluation (text or audio) from the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the breakdown" icon="list-checks">
    See scores per dimension and where to improve, then refine the agent and re-check.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Evaluation is the quality loop behind [how Intrlume works](/how-intrlume-works) — measure, fix, and run again.
</Callout>
