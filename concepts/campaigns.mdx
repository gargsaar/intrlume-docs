---
title: Campaigns
description: Run calls to an audience on a schedule, with retries and concurrency — in Intrlume or from your CRM.
---

A **campaign** runs your [agent](/concepts/agents) against an [audience](/concepts/audiences) on a schedule you control. You can launch a campaign **inside your CRM** or in Intrlume.

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick audience + agent" icon="users">
    Choose one or more audiences and the agent that will make the calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the window" icon="calendar">
    Choose the date range, days, hours, and timezone calls are allowed to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set retries & pace" icon="refresh-cw">
    Decide how often to retry (for example, unanswered only) and how many calls run at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run and manage

A campaign starts as a **draft**, then moves through **active → paused / resumed → completed**. Watch live progress — calls made, answer rate — and pause or stop at any time.

<Callout kind="info">
  Calls only dial inside your scheduled window and respect your concurrency limit, so you stay compliant and within capacity.
</Callout>

Review outcomes per campaign, or across everything in [Call Logs](/concepts/call-logs) and [Call Analytics](/concepts/call-analytics).
