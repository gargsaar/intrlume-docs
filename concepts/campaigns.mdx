A **campaign** is a scheduled batch of outbound calls. Intrlume takes one [agent](/concepts/agents) and one or more contact lists, then calls everyone for you — on the schedule you set, with retries.

## What a campaign needs

| Item | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Audience | The contacts to call — one or more lists |
| Agent | The voice agent that makes the calls |
| Phone number | A caller number for outbound dialing |
| Schedule + timezone | When calls are allowed to run |
| Retry rule | Whether, and how often, to try again |

<Callout kind="info">
  Campaigns place **outbound** calls, so they need a provisioned phone number and a plan beyond the free trial (the trial is web-only).
</Callout>

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basics" icon="megaphone">
    Name the campaign, pick one or more audiences, and choose the agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duration" icon="calendar">
    Set the start and end dates — calls only run inside this window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule & retries" icon="refresh-cw">
    Choose the days, hours, and timezone calls may run. Set how often to retry (every few days) and the retry mode — **all** leads or **unanswered only**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Draft | Created, not started yet |
| Scheduled | Waiting for its start date |
| Active | Calls are being made |
| Paused | Calling is paused; resume anytime |
| Completed | Finished, or reached its end date |

You can **pause and resume**, **end** early, **rerun** a finished campaign, and **exclude** specific contacts you don't want called.

## Scheduling & retries

Calls only dial inside your schedule window (days, hours, timezone) and are paced to stay within telephony limits. Outside the window the campaign **pauses and picks up automatically** in the next slot. With **unanswered-only** retries, Intrlume skips anyone who already answered and re-tries the rest every few days until the end date.

## Track a campaign

Watch live progress — calls made and answer rate — and dig into per-campaign analytics: rounds, completed / failed / no-answer counts, average duration, and a per-contact history with recordings and summaries.

## Run from your CRM

Launch a campaign straight from your CRM. In a list view in **Zoho, Freshworks, or Bigin**, select the contacts, pick an agent and a schedule, and Intrlume runs a paced campaign over them — every result (disposition, summary, and recording) is written back to the matching record.
