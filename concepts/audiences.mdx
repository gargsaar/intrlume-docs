An **audience** is a list of contacts (leads) you want to call. Build one once and reuse it across [campaigns](/concepts/campaigns).

## Build an audience

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload a CSV" icon="upload">
    Import your contacts from a spreadsheet. Intrlume maps the columns (name, phone, email…) and standardizes phone numbers for you.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From your CRM" icon="users">
    Bring contacts in from your connected CRM instead of a spreadsheet.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Leads and custom fields

Each contact is a **lead** with a phone number and any **custom fields** you include — account number, appointment time, amount due. The agent uses those fields on the call, so a greeting like "Hi {name}, your demo is at {time}" is personalized to each contact.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Clean phone numbers and clear column names import best. Duplicates are flagged during upload.
</Callout>

After a run, review per-contact outcomes in [Call Logs](/concepts/call-logs).
