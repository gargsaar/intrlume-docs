**Call Analytics** rolls up every call into a performance dashboard, so you can see how your agents and campaigns are doing at a glance.

## Key metrics

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Volume & outcomes" icon="bar-chart">
    Total calls, plus answered, voicemail, no answer, busy, and failed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Productivity" icon="activity">
    Productive vs short calls, average talk time, and total talk time.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Trends and breakdowns

See calls over time, your busiest hours, call-duration distribution, hangup analysis, per-campaign comparisons, and breakdowns by voice and language. Filter by time range and direction.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Watch answer rate and productive-call share to judge list quality and agent performance — not just raw volume.
</Callout>
