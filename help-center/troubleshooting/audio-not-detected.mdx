---
title: Audio Not Detected
description: Fix microphone input issues during a web call test.
---

## Symptoms

- A **web call** test doesn't pick up your voice.
- The agent doesn't respond to anything you say.

## Common causes

- The browser blocked microphone access.
- The wrong input device is selected, or no device is picking up sound.

## Fix it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Allow microphone access" icon="mic">
    Grant microphone access when the browser prompts you. If you dismissed it, re-enable the mic for the site in your browser's site settings, then reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your input device" icon="settings">
    In your system sound settings, confirm the right microphone is selected and the input level moves when you speak.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload and retry" icon="refresh-cw">
    Refresh the Intrlume tab and start the web call again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Still no audio? Try a different browser (Chrome works best) and make sure no other app — a meeting or recorder — is holding the microphone.
</Callout>
