---
title: Inaccurate KB answers
description: When your agent answers from a knowledge base incorrectly, check it's linked to the agent, then review and re-index the content.
---

## Symptoms

- The agent gives **wrong, outdated, or incomplete** answers from a knowledge base.
- It misses details that are clearly in your original document — or ignores the knowledge base entirely.

## Why this happens

There are two common causes:

1. The knowledge base **isn't linked to the agent**, so the agent never uses it.
2. The knowledge base is linked, but the **indexed content** — the text the agent actually retrieves — is wrong, incomplete, or stale.

Work through them in order.

## 1. Check the knowledge base is linked to the agent

The agent only uses knowledge bases that are attached to it in the [workflow editor](/guides/configure-your-agent). If it isn't linked, the content doesn't matter — the agent never sees it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the workflow editor" icon="workflow">
    Open the agent and go to its workflow editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Knowledge Base panel" icon="book-open">
    In the left sidebar, click **Knowledge Base** to expand the panel. Any linked knowledge bases are listed there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link it if it's missing" icon="link">
    If you see **"No KB linked"**, click **Link** and choose the knowledge base from the list. (Only knowledge bases that have finished processing appear.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the workflow" icon="save">
    Click **Save** at the top right. Linking only takes effect once the workflow is saved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Re-test the agent. If it's linked and answers are still off, fix the content below.

## 2. Review and fix the indexed content

Your agent doesn't re-read the original file on every call — it retrieves from the **indexed content** that was extracted when the knowledge base was processed. If extraction missed a section, garbled a table, or the source has since changed, the answers will be off.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the knowledge base" icon="book-open">
    Go to **Knowledge Base** in the sidebar and click the knowledge base's name to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the indexed content" icon="file-text">
    The viewer shows the **indexed content** — exactly what the agent retrieves on a call. Compare it against your source; for a PDF, use **View original** to open the uploaded file side by side. Look for missing sections, garbled tables, or extra clutter (navigation, repeated headers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes" icon="pencil">
    Edit the text directly in the viewer — correct mistakes, add missing details, and remove noise. An **Unsaved changes** note appears as you edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Re-index" icon="refresh-cw">
    Click **Save & Re-index**. Your edits are re-chunked and re-embedded — you'll see **Re-indexing started**, and the status returns to **Ready** in a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-test the agent" icon="play">
    Ask the agent the same question again to confirm the answer is now correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  For a **website** knowledge base that's gone stale, use **Recrawl** (in the ⋯ menu) to pull fresh content from the URL. Note: recrawling **replaces** your manual edits with the newly scraped text.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="info">
  Only a knowledge base in the **Ready** state is used on calls. If it's stuck on **Processing**, **Indexing**, or shows **Failed**, wait for it to finish — or re-upload — before testing.
</Callout>

For how knowledge bases are extracted, chunked, and retrieved, see [Knowledge Base](/concepts/knowledge-base).
