## What is Intrlume?

Intrlume is a self-serve platform for building **voice AI call agents** — AI that holds real phone conversations. Describe what you want in plain language and Intrlume generates a working agent in minutes. Your agents make **outbound** calls, answer **inbound** calls, and talk to visitors on your **website** — with built-in evaluation and strong support for Indian languages and noisy, real-world calls.

## Key capabilities

| Capability | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Channels | Outbound, inbound, and web calls |
| Languages | 12+ Indian languages, tuned for noisy, real-world calls |
| Agent creation | Natural-language generation, refined in a visual workflow editor |
| Call quality | Built-in evaluation on every call (faithfulness, hallucination, topic adherence) |
| CRM | Native integration with Freshworks, Zoho, and Bigin |
| Concurrency | Scales with your plan |
