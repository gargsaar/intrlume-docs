## What is Intrlume?

Intrlume is a self-serve platform for building **voice AI call agents** — AI that holds real phone conversations. Describe what you want in plain language and Intrlume generates a working agent in minutes. Your agents make **outbound** calls, answer **inbound** calls, and talk to visitors on your **website** — with built-in evaluation and strong support for Indian languages and noisy, real-world calls.
